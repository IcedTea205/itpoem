• HU TAO •

Fine as cocoa seeds you sow.

Mistaken spirit blooming in the quip

You're in a nutshell, not to boredom known,

And lightly hovering with acute grip.

Your gentle wings embrace us with warm fire

And smile, cute riddle of a joy you own,

The game and heaven only you desire.



Comes the night. Of one colour swarms in sight

Which is dull. Nor will I attempt to hull,

It lights not. Inward lured, you too have got

Charon here. He and she the souls with care

Lead to cross. With great dignity for loss,

You will wait. Nor the last farewell you'll hate.

Not to say goodbye you vow.





• My Dearest Friend •

The daughter of Hu came into being, all the world now to be seeing.

Flame butterflies flutter like falling plum petals, in a transient dream a fragrance settles.

The wind rises, the clouds drift, the impermanence of life is a gift.

The flowers bloom the mountains still, and with it the truth reveals.
